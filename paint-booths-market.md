<p><p>The paint booths market is primarily categorized into open type and closed type paint booths. Open type paint booths are designed for smaller applications, featuring minimal containment and often used for quick jobs. They facilitate airflow but may lack strict environmental controls. In contrast, closed type paint booths provide a controlled environment, incorporating filtration and exhaust systems to manage overspray and emissions effectively. These booths are ideal for larger operations requiring precision, compliance with regulations, and improved finish quality.</p></p>
