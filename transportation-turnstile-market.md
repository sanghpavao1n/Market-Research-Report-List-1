<p><p>The transportation turnstile market features two main types: optical turnstiles and arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles use advanced sensor technology to detect passing individuals, providing a seamless entry experience while ensuring security. They are often used in high-traffic areas and offer aesthetic appeal. In contrast, arm turnstiles utilize physical barriers that rotate to allow entry upon proper authorization, adding a layer of security. These turnstiles are commonly employed in transit systems and secured entrances, balancing efficiency with safety.</p></p>
