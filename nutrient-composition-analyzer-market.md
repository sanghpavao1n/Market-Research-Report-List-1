<p><p>The Nutrient Composition Analyzer market consists of various technologies, notably DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology is used for precise fat and bone density measurements in biological samples, making it valuable for health assessments. In contrast, NIR technology employs light absorption principles to quickly analyze the nutrient content in food and agricultural products without damaging them. Both methods enable efficient nutrient profiling, aiding industries focused on food quality and health monitoring.</p></p>
