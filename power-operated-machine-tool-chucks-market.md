<p><p>The power-operated machine tool chucks market comprises three main types: 2-jaw, 3-jaw, and 4-jaw chucks. </p><p>2-jaw chucks are ideal for grabbing irregularly shaped objects and provide a secure grip. </p><p>3-jaw chucks offer uniform clamping on cylindrical items, making them popular for general machining tasks. </p><p>4-jaw chucks are versatile and allow for independent adjustment of each jaw, enabling precision gripping of non-symmetrical workpieces. Each type serves specific applications in machining for enhanced efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
