<p><p>The Acoustic Hailing Devices market is categorized into two main types: Short Range Acoustic Hailing Devices (SHAHDs) and Long Range Acoustic Hailing Devices (LRAHDs). SHAHDs are designed for close-quarters communication, typically effective up to a few hundred meters, enabling clear vocal instructions or warnings in tactical situations. In contrast, LRAHDs extend this capability over several kilometers, allowing operators to project messages over greater distances for crowd control, maritime safety, or military operations, enhancing situational awareness and effective communication.</p></p>
