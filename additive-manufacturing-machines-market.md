<p><p>The Additive Manufacturing Machines market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)** uses thermoplastic filaments, layering material to create models.</p><p>2. **SLA (Stereolithography)** employs UV light to cure liquid resin into solid forms.</p><p>3. **DLP (Digital Light Processing)** is similar to SLA but uses digital light for faster production.</p><p>4. **MJ (Material Jetting)** sprays droplets of material to build layers.</p><p>5. **SLS (Selective Laser Sintering)** employs lasers to fuse powdered materials.</p><p>6. **Others** include various niche technologies and customized applications.</p></p>
